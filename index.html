Golf

I’ve been playing golf for 8 years, and it’s a sport that makes me feel free and connected with nature, which is incredibly comforting in today’s world. One of my most memorable experiences was a two-week training stage in Scotland at St Andrews, the “Home of Golf.” Walking the historic fairways where the game was born was truly inspiring. I also admire great players like Tiger Woods, Rory McIlroy, and Jon Rahm. Tiger Woods, with his 15 major championships and 82 PGA Tour wins, has set numerous records. Rory McIlroy, known for his powerful drives, has won four major championships and is a three-time FedEx Cup champion. Jon Rahm, with his recent Masters and U.S. Open victories, continues to impress with his consistency and skill. Among these greats, my favorite is Tiger Woods.
